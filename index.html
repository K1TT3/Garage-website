Spotlight

Welcome to the spotlight section. This is the place where I present to you each month a new car from my garage.
This month I present to you the 2018 BMW M2 Competition, the little brother of the M3 and M4.

2 mighty 4 you

The M2 Competition is powered by the S55, the 3 litre twin turbo inline six found in the last generation M3 and M4. It delivers 410hp and 550nm to the rear wheels.

Suit up

This M2 has the carbon fiber front splitter, carbon fiber mirror caps, carbon fiber roof and the m performance bonnet.

Beauty from behind

From the rear the M2 just looks stunning. It has the LCI tail lights, which I find visually more appealing then the ones from the early model. Add the carbon fiber ducktail and the Akrapovic exhaust and it is a real eye catcher.
